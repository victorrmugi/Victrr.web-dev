MUAMMAR GADDAFI

  • ➡️Muammar Gaddafi was a Libyan revolutionary and political leader
  • ➡️He came to power through a military coup in 1969, overthrowing King Idris
  • ➡️He ruled Libya for over four decades until his death in 2011
  • ➡️He was known for his authoritarian rule and controversial policies
  • ➡️ Gaddafi was killed by the NTC on 20 October 2011 .
  • ➡️His legacy remains highly debated

    • Biography

    Muammar Gaddafi, the former leader of Libya, was killed by the National Transitional Council (NTC) on 20 October 2011 after the Battle of Sirte. Gaddafi was captured by NTC forces and summarily executed shortly afterwards.[1] The NTC initially claimed Gaddafi succumbed to injuries sustained in a firefight when loyalist forces attempted to free him, although a video of his last moments shows rebel fighters dragging Gaddafi out of a drainage pipe, beating him and one of them sodomizing him with a bayonet[2] before he was shot several times.[3] The killing of Gaddafi was criticized as a violation of international law.[4][5] Amnesty International and Human Rights Watch called for an independent autopsy and an investigation into how Gaddafi died.[6]>
